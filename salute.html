Salute Part 1

A collaborative performance piece with Alice Zhang and Dan Dao. The only score was to embody Asian Rage by screaming and then taking up as much space as possible

Salute Part 2

A dance piece choreographed by Sheena and performed by them alongside Jenny Huang and Natasha Toomey. It further explores Asian Rage through the lens of micro and macro storytelling.

Salute Part 3
